Instructions

Welcome to the Game of Pig. To win, be the player with the most points at the end of the game. The game ends at the end of a round where at least one player has 100 or more points.
On each turn, you may press 'Roll Die' as many times as you like to obtain more points. However, if you roll a 1, your turn is over, and you do not obtain any points that turn.
To end your turn and keep the points you have, press the 'End Turn' button.
